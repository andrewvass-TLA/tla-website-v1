AI-Driven Financial Review

Build your year by the numbers.

Tell it what you want to earn, how many hours you want to work, and how many weeks off. It builds the entire financial picture of your business — what your time is worth, how many loans you need, and exactly where every one comes from.

STEP 01

Know your hourly rate

Income goal + hours + vacation → your true rate of pay. The number that changes how you spend every hour.

STEP 02

Find your break-even

Expenses + tax bracket → the exact number of loans you must close each month just to break even.

STEP 03

Map every loan to a source

Database, partners, consumer-direct. How many leads from each, and the conversion rate to hit your goal.

STEP 04

Track yourself live

Every lead and closing feeds your numbers in real time — batting average, income, hourly rate. Adjust before the year gets away.

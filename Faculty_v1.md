## **About This Page**

### **What this page is for**

The Faculty page answers the \#1 credibility question a prospect asks before buying a coaching platform: *who am I actually going to learn from?*

It is not a top-of-funnel persuasion page. A visitor who lands here has already been intrigued elsewhere on the site and is now validating whether The Loan Atlas faculty is worth their time and money. The page's job is to prove — clearly and specifically — that every faculty member is an active producer, division president, or strategist with real-world credibility, not a retired coach selling advice from the sidelines.

The anchor positioning for this page is: **Originators first. Coaches second.**

### **Where it fits in the site**

Most visitors arrive here from the homepage's primary nav, from a "meet the faculty" link on the What's Inside page, or from a late-page CTA on Why The Loan Atlas. The page hands off to two next steps — a free coaching session for the reader who wants to talk through fit, and direct membership signup for the reader who finishes the page convinced.

The flow is: **Homepage (or elsewhere) → Faculty → Join The Loan Atlas** (primary) or **Build Your Growth Plan** (secondary).

### **Who this page is written for**

Any visitor doing due diligence on who they'd be learning from. Default assumption: a mid-career loan officer evaluating whether The Loan Atlas faculty has anything to teach someone already successful. The page should also work for newer originators (who need to believe the faculty can meet them where they are) and veterans (who need to see names and track records they respect).

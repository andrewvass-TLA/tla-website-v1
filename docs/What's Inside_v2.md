# **The Loan Atlas \- What's Inside Page**

## **About This Page**

### **What this page is for**

The What's Inside page answers one specific question: "What exactly do I get?" It lives one step downstream from the homepage — its job is not to persuade from scratch, but to give serious prospects the clarity and confidence to act.

A visitor arrives here after the homepage has already done its work. They understand the problem. They believe a system exists. Now they want to know what's actually inside before they commit. This page gives them that — clearly, specifically, and without overselling.

### **Where it fits in the site**

The homepage establishes the operating system positioning and introduces the five AI-powered systems at a high level. It splits traffic between individual loan officers and organizational buyers, then moves both toward a next step.

The What's Inside page is the primary next step for individual loan officers. It expands on what the homepage introduced — AI tools, training framework, live coaching, scripts and templates, community — with enough detail to answer every "but what does that actually mean for my business?" question a prospect might have.

The flow is: **Homepage → What's Inside → Schedule Free Coaching Session or Become a Member.**

### **Who this page is written for**

The default audience is the mid-career loan officer — someone with 5–15 years of experience who is working hard, producing inconsistently, and knows something is missing. The language and proof points should resonate most strongly with this persona, while remaining accessible to newer originators and veterans alike.

### **Tone and voice notes**

This page is explanation-first, not persuasion-first. The reader already believes there's a problem worth solving — they don't need to be convinced again. The job is to make them feel like they're getting a clear, honest tour from someone who has been in the seat and knows exactly what they need.

Each section opens with the reader's experience before introducing the product. The product is always the answer to something real, not a feature being listed for its own sake. Proof is woven throughout each section — placed at the moment of highest skepticism, not piled at the bottom.

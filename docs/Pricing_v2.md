## **About This Page**

### **What this page is for**

The Pricing page handles the "how much is it?" question at the bottom of the funnel. A visitor arrives here having already been sold on the why — from the homepage, What's Inside, or a referral. Its job is not to persuade from scratch. Its job is to make the price transparent, the options clear, the savings obvious, and give organizational buyers a clean exit to their own path.

### **Where it fits in the site**

The Pricing page is a late-funnel decision surface for individual loan officers. Most visitors arrive from the primary nav or from a late-page CTA on the homepage or What's Inside. The primary next step is membership signup (monthly or annual). The secondary next step is a free consultation for prospects who want to speak with the team before committing. Organizational buyers are routed to the Corporate page.

The flow is: **Homepage or What's Inside → Pricing → Become a Member** (or **Book a Free Consultation** as the fallback).

### **Who this page is written for**

Individual loan officers who are close to deciding. They've likely seen the homepage and probably What's Inside. They're not looking for re-persuasion on the why — they're doing the math on whether this fits their business.

## **Common Questions**

**What's the difference between monthly and annual?** Both plans are 12-month memberships with identical access — same AI systems, same coaching, same library, same community. The only difference is how you pay. Annual is paid upfront and saves you $698 (the equivalent of two months free). Monthly spreads the same annual commitment across 12 payments of $349.

**Is there a contract or long-term commitment?** Yes — both plans are 12-month memberships. We built it that way on purpose. Real transformation in a mortgage business doesn't happen in 30 days. It happens over a full year of coaching, installation, and execution. A month-to-month subscription would let you bail before the work actually pays off, and that's not the outcome we want for members.

**Can I switch from monthly to annual later?** Yes. Many members start with monthly billing and move to annual at renewal once they've seen the platform pay for itself.

**Do you offer team or enterprise pricing?** Yes — for branches, divisions, and full organizations. [Explore Enterprise Pricing →](https://claude.ai/organizations)

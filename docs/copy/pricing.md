## Frequently Asked Questions

**What's the difference between monthly and annual?**

Both plans are 12-month memberships with identical access — same AI systems, same coaching, same library, same community. The only difference is how you pay. Annual is paid upfront and saves you $698 (the equivalent of two months free). Monthly spreads the same annual commitment across 12 payments of $349.

**Is there a contract or long-term commitment?**

Yes — both plans are 12-month memberships. We built it that way on purpose. Real transformation in a mortgage business doesn't happen in 30 days. It happens over a full year of coaching, installation, and execution. A month-to-month subscription would let you bail before the work actually pays off, and that's not the outcome we want for members.

**Can I switch from monthly to annual later?**

Yes. Many members start with monthly billing and move to annual at renewal once they've seen the platform pay for itself.

## The Reason Your Marketing Keeps Getting Ignored

Your clients and partners are being bombarded with marketing messages all day, every day — and their brains have built a filter against most of it.

Most mortgage marketing hits that filter and bounces: emails deleted on subject line alone, flyers tossed without a glance, social posts scrolled past in half a second.

### Pull statement

The marketing that breaks through the noise is built on a specific set of principles most loan officers have never been taught.

### Comparison

#### Marketing That Gets Ignored

- Talks about the lender instead of the client
- Opens with a generic intro nobody cares about
- Makes claims with no proof to back them up
- Buries the offer in vague, slow-feeling promises
- Loaded with jargon and complicated next steps

#### Marketing That Converts

- Speaks directly to the client, not about you
- Opens with a problem they're already aware of
- Builds proof into every asset
- Frames the dream outcome around speed and certainty
- Strips out the jargon and friction

## **About This Page**

### **What this page is for**

This is a landing-page-style corporate sales page — a single concentrated persuasion arc for branch managers, division presidents, owners, and executives. It does not mirror the homepage's structure, because leaders who arrive here from the homepage need to feel they're being spoken to differently, not the same content with "team" pasted in.

The central tension is the one Josh Mettle names in the *AI-Empowered Mortgage Leader Masterplan*: every leader is now managing two kinds of originators — has-beens and will-be's — and the gap between them is widening every month. The job of this page is to make the leader feel that gap on their P\&L, reframe what's actually causing it, and position The Loan Atlas as the only system that closes it across a team.

### **Where it fits in the site**

The homepage splits traffic at "Who We Serve," routing organizational buyers here. This is the terminus for Funnel 2\. The reader arrives already aware their team is uneven — they don't need to be sold on the existence of an organizational performance gap. They need to be sold on The Loan Atlas as the right way to close it.

### **Tone and voice notes**

Structural, capability-forward, "your team" throughout. Leader-to-leader register. The problem section is in the leader's own internal monologue (their P\&L, their team meeting, their roster). The capability sections are confident and operational — installation language, not transformation language. Aggregate proof. Consultative CTA throughout.

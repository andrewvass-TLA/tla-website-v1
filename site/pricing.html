Frequently asked questions

Can I change plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Prorated charges apply on upgrades; downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees on Solo or Team plans. Enterprise rollouts may include a one-time integration fee depending on scope.
Do I need to install anything?
No. The Loan Atlas runs entirely in your browser. Optional integrations with your LOS take a few clicks.
What if I'm part of a brokerage already?
Most members keep their current brokerage relationship. The Loan Atlas operates as a productivity layer on top of whatever LOS or CRM you use today.
How long until I see results?
Most members report measurable wins (recovered hours, faster follow-up, new closed loans) within the first 30 days. The full system compounds over 90 days.
